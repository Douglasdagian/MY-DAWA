ABOUT US

My dawa,is a website or rather an organisation which delivers drugs to the HIV positive patients to their homes who shy away from getting drugs from the hospital by ordering them through our website.

We also give tips to the patients on how to live a healthy and happy life while under still medication.

We also try to create awareness and educate the society on how to deal with HIV positive patients and to treat them with love and care.And also the dangers of contracting HIV/AIDS VIRUS.

TIPS

Eat a diet high in vegetables,fruits,wholegrains,and legumes.

Exercise regularly.

Avoid using drugs.

Take your medication on time

Visit your doctor for checkups.

Drink alot of water.
